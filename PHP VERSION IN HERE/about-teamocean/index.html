About Team Ocean

Team Ocean is the world’s leading waste collection and recycling company.

The wave trademark of Team Ocean is one of the most recognized logos in the global waste collection and recycling communities.

With a fan base that spans every continent, the company has designed and built the world’s largest recycling-focused ecosystem of hardware, software and services.

Team Ocean’s award-winning hardware includes high-performance waste sorting machine. Team Ocean’s software platform, with over 100 million users, includes Ocean Synapse (an AI to sort waste), Ocean Chroma™ (a proprietary RGB lighting technology system), and Ocean Cortex (a AI waste finder for underwater robots).

In services, Ocean Clear is one of the world’s most effective ocean cleaning services.

Founded in 2021 and dual-headquartered in China and Singapore, Team Ocean has 17 offices worldwide and is recognized as the leading company for waste collection and recycling in the USA, Europe and China.
