Frequently Asked Questions

Q : Where do the donations go to and what is it spent on?
A : Straight into the company's funds, to be used for cleaning projects and paying trash collectors to help us out.
Q : Does the company use volunteers or employees for cleanups?
A : We hire homeless people and provide them a job with decent wage, we also get volunteers from agencys to help out as we need as much help we need.
Q : Are the rumours of the company is using slave labour in China true?
A : Our lawyer has advised against answering such a question.
Q : What is the company's plan after all oceans are clean?
A : Our plan would be to shift our focus to recycling and find solutions to aid cleanup projects.
Q : Why are the links blog and feedback only shown here.
A : Because we don't want internet trolls with nothing better to do spamming them.
Q : Would a world where all oceans be clean be possible?
A : Yes, no and kinda. As long as terrible people exist and litter at beaches or dump toxic waste into earth's water bodies. Clean oceans will remain to be just a myth.
